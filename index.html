MoPSoS

Database of Ancient Greek Morphology, Phonology, Syntax, and Scansion

MoPSoS is an open-access digital corpus of Archaic Greek works that can be searched for linguistic sub-categories not otherwise encoded in similar databases, like morphemes, phonological and syntactic structures, and meter.

MoPSoS is a work in progress being built by PhD students to answer research questions about the Ancient Greek language. The collection of texts in the database is under development.
