Morphology

Browse and query the morphologically annotated corpus — every token tagged for part of speech, case, number, tense, mood, voice, gender, and degree — and chart how those features are distributed.

Morphology tab

There are three ways to explore the corpus:

  • Quick filter — pick a part of speech and MOPSOS shows only the grammatical features that actually apply to it, each offering only the values that occur. Results are paginated 50 per page, and columns that don't apply are hidden automatically.
  • Custom queries — tucked inside the Quick filter, for writing your own read-only queries by hand. Press Enter to run.
  • Explore & visualize — count tokens by any dimension (book, lemma, case…), optionally broken down by a second dimension, under any filter — e.g. which books have the most aorist optatives, or how noun number and case combine.
